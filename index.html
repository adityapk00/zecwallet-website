Zecwallet Lite


Zecwallet Lite is a fully featured shielded wallet for Zcash.

Syncs in under a minute. No blockchain download needed!


Download

Private By Default

Send fully private, fully shielded transactions with ease!

  • Shileded and Transparent addresses

    Full support for incoming and outgoing, z-addresses and t-addresses.
  • Full Memo support

    Full support for incoming and outgoing memos
  • Android Companion App

    Connect your Zecwallet Lite to your Android phone and send and recieve fully shielded transactions from your phone.
  • Encrypt Your Wallet

    Encrypt your private keys to protect your wallet. Zecwallet Lite uses Viewkeys to sync the chain to keep your funds safe!

More Platforms And Binaries

Explore more Zecwallet Projects

Zecwallet Fullnode

Zecwallet UI that runs against your local Zcash full node. Can run over Tor for additional privacy. Comes with an embedded Zcashd full node.
 

Paper Wallet

Offline capable Sapling and Transparent address and private key generator. Runs on desktops, chromebooks, Raspbery Pi and more devices.

Command Line Lightwallet

Command line light client for Zcash that can run on a variety of platforms. Fully scriptable.
 

Companion App for Android

Connect to your Zecwallet Lite or Fullnode from your Android phone, and securely send shielded and transparent transactions from your phone.

Zecwallet Lite is an open source project with a permissive MIT license and is supported by a grant from the Zcash Foundation.
