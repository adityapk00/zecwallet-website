Zecwallet FullNode

Zecwallet FullNode is a UI frontend wallet for the zcashd full node. Embedded full node included!

Full Privacy. Full Control.

Send and Receive shielded and shielded transactions via your own full node.

Your Own Full Node

Run your own full node, and send and recieve transactions via your own full node to completely be in control of your own privacy.

Connect over Tor

Optionally connect over Tor for an even more private zcash experience.

Own your own private keys

Your private keys are completely on your own node and in your control. Import and Export your private keys as you will.

Embedded Zcash Node

Run your own external node or let Zecwallet start it's embedded node managed by Zecwallet.

Fast Sync

Zecwallet's embedded node features Fast Sync, which can sync the zcash blockchain upto 33% faster than an external node.

Zecwallet Fullnode

Zecwallet UI that runs against your local Zcash full node. Can run over Tor for additional privacy. Comes with an embedded Zcashd full node.
 

Paper Wallet

Offline capable Sapling and Transparent address and private key generator. Runs on desktops, chromebooks, Raspbery Pi and more devices.

Zecwallet Lite

A fully featured light client with full support for shielded addresses including memos. Get started in seconds.
 

Companion App for Android

Connect to your Zecwallet Lite or Fullnode from your Android phone, and securely send shielded and transparent transactions from your phone.
